Welcome to an all-out
Marvel brawl!

Use your type advantages and
raw power to eliminate your
opposition. Be the last one
standing and claim victory!

Getting Started

Press the Draw Cards button
to generate two random decks.
Player 1's cards will populate in
the bottom pane and Player 2's
will populate on top.

Tips & Rules

Click on a card in your deck to
choose a hero or villian, then
click an opposing card to
select a target. Lastly, press
the Action button to engage.

Multiplayer and Types

Play will alternate on each Action by
swapping the top and bottom panes.
Simply trade mouse control on each turn
to keep the action going!
